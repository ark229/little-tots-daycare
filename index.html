LITTLE TOTS DAYCARE

If you are looking for more than just childcare, you have come to the right place! With an average of over 15 years of experience among our teaching staff, your child will have a great opportunity to learn and grow at our center. We work hard to include each child's family in all that we do! So, plan on feeling welcomed into the Little Tots family. We are excited to meet you!

Early childhood is the best time to learn!

Right now, your child's brain is growing faster than it ever will in their whole life, making amazing connections every second. That's why every learning moment counts! Our classrooms are designed around your child's unique needs, and our curriculum is built from the latest research in early childhood development to help little minds grow.

VISIT THE DAYCARE

Are you interested in enrolling your little one at Little Tots Daycare but, you would like to tour our facilities first? Just contact us, and we'll schedule a visit.

MEET OUR STAFF

Our teachers and staff are dedicated to providing a safe, nurturing environment that cultivates self-esteem, creativity, and growth.

VOLUNTEER AT LITTLE TOTS

Our volunteers are people who dedicate their time and energy while also having the opportunity to make an important difference in the lives of our children. This voluntary role requires hard work, but it is rewarding for people who enjoy interacting with babies, toddlers, and children. Our dedicated volunteers are here to guide our Litte Tots as they grow and learn. Volunteers usually perform a variety of duties as they assist the daycare supervisor, teachers, or primary caretakers. If you would like to volunteer at Little Tots Daycare Center please click the link below. Your service is welcomed and appreciated!
